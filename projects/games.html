Once upon a time...

there was a boy named Link. He lived by himself, with friends, or family in a small village. Life was normal until Link was selected to continue the long time honored tradition of wearing the "Heroe's Clothes" for the annual festival celebrating a great hero that vanquished darkness many years ago. Again and again, this celebration is held and each time a new darkness arises which Link must then set out from his tiny village to destroy, all while trying to save a princess. A few big monsters, some triforces, treasures, a couple instruments, and melodies later the darkness is put to sleep... only to awaken once more 100 years later and to be fought back by the same namesake...

This is...
THE LEGEND OF LINK ZELDA!
