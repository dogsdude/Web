The Gameplay....

in the Legend of Zelda usually revolves around adventuring the land of Hyrule. You are sometimes accompanied by your horse, Epona, other times you ride across the land on a bird, boat or train, and still others, you transform into a wolf or warp from point to point. Ultimately, you will travel across the land helping others and getting past some sort of trouble or puzzle they have for you before entering into a dungeon. Dungeons usually are combat focused areas that introduce you to a new item that helps you progress through the game and unlocks areas that were previously inaccessible. These typically end in a large boss fight that tests your ability to use the item that the dungeon has given you. Then you exit the dungeon, rinse and repeat! Many of the games follow a linear fashion where you need to progress through the dungeons in a certain order, but more and more they are becoming non-linear allowing you to explore whereever you see fit (even if that means fighting the final boss first!) Gameplay is by far the reason people keep coming back to the games over and over. It's just that good.
